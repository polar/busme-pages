Welcome to Busme!



As a transit system operator, would you like to let your customers know where your buses are in real time? If they can find the bus they want on their smart phone, do you think that would that increase your ridership?

As a rider, would you like to know where the buses are in your town? Does your bus company not foster that capability on-line, or doesn't want to? Then you can do it yourself with Busme!

Busme can help you get that effort of the ground. Our service lets you set up your own route based transportation site that lets you monitor your transport vehicles in a web browser or with our Android Phone App.

We supply only the service, and you acquire all your own equipment and hardware. In fact, the only equipment you need is a smart cell phone with Global Positioning System (GPS) location capability, which you may already have! You do need a wireless data plan for the phone, and, of course, you need wireless data/phone service in your area.

Getting Started.

Getting started is relatively easy. You don't have to be a computer programmer or even a transportation expert, but you do need to have some computer skills. Essentially, you need to be able to drop and move icons around on a map and be able to use a basic spreadsheet program such as, MS Excel or LibreOffice Calc.

Please Excuse Our Looks

Busme is a really new concept. Although there are competitors out there that can get your systems on line, we provide a service to let you perform this function yourself, without great cost or special equipment. You draw your bus routes; you supply the stops and their times; and you deploy the application into a running state, and change service dates.

This site and concept is very new and we'd like your experimentation and feedback on how to make it better. You save all your work on your local machines, so you may restore it. This may give you some confidence into our newish state. So, please start experimenting.

Busme is Social

Busme has a great hidden social component to it. For example, you have a transit system in your neighborhood, but the operator doesn't have Busme. No problem. The only piece of equipment you need is an Android Phone with GPS and a wireless data plan and somebody to be on the bus! That means, you don't have to be the driver, you can just be a rider! If enough riders play the game of reporting their locations while on the bus, then everybody gets to know where the bus is. It just takes a bit of effort from a concerted individual to draw the stops and routes, enter in the schedule data, and run the site.

Get started today. Sign in above, create a New Site, and you will be logged directly into your own site, and start managing your route system.
