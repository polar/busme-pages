Get Your Buses Online

Transit Operations

As a transit system operator, would you like to let your customers know where your buses are in real time? If they can find the bus they want on their smart phone, do you think that would increase your ridership?

Would you like to get this capability for a low cost monthly fee instead of hiring transportation information companies to develop specialized services for you?

Busme! can help you get that effort off the ground. The Busme! service builds your own transportation site. This site gives you the ability to model and monitor your route based transport vehicles. You may monitor the locations and routes of your buses on this website, or with our Busme! Android App.

You maintain your own equipment, which are Android phones or tablets, and you just sign up for the Busme! services. It is as simple as that.

See where Busme! is operating. Go to our map page. Click Here.
