Quick Help for Busme! Android Version 0.32.6+

New Feature: Shaking
Shaking the device will make the Routes List slide out of the way or back in. See "Swipe Right on the Routes List"
SingleTap on Map
  • If performed on a bus blip, the app will focus its attention on that bus, removing all others from view. It will turn the route and blip green.
  • If performed on a route path, the app will remove all routes and buses that do not pass through that point.
  • Otherwise, this gesture brings up the zoom in/out buttons.
Long Press on the Map
  • If performed on a bus blip, it will try to bring up a dialog for reporting locations on that bus. This requires that you log on and if you have not logged on before or in a while, you will be brought to an authorization page.
  • Otherwise, it is ignored
Double Tap on the Map
This gesture will center the map on your location. This is how you can find yourself on the map if you flung the map too far away.
Press and Drag on the Map
This gesture will drag the map around with your finger.
Pinch or Spread with two fingers on the Map
These two finger gestures will zoom the map in and out, respectively, in the general vicinity of your touches.
Single Tap on the Routes List
  • If looking at just the bus routes, and not the actual journeys, this gesture will go into a mode where you are viewing only buses on that route, or just the paths of the route if there are no buses. The map will center on the extent of the paths on the route, trying to encompass them all in a single view.
  • If looking at just buses on a single route, this gesture will go into a mode where you are looking at only the bus you selected, as if you single tapped on its blip. (see above).
  • If you are looking at only one bus that is green, this gesture will center the map on the current location of the bus.
Long Press on the Routes List
  • If looking at just routes, this gesture will highlight the route and any buses on that route in red for five seconds.
  • If looking at just one route, this gesture will highlight the route and the bus you selected in red and center the map on its position.
  • If looking at just one bus in green, this gesture will center the map on the bus' location.
Swipe Right on the Routes List
This gesture will move the Routes List out of the way and expose a small button in the upper right hand corner of the screen. You click on this button to get the Routes List back.
The Back Button
The Back Button is used to back out of your various bus selections. Usually you start in SHOW_ALL, which shows all the routes and buses, but only the route names. After a single ta on the Routes List, you progress to SHOW_ROUTE, which shows just the paths and buses of a single route, If you tap on the routes list again on a particular line you progress to SHOW_VEHICLE, which is just displaying a single bus, in which its path and blip are in green. You may make different bus selections in between based on a single tap on the map that may exclude some routes.These selections stack up in such a way that you may use the back button to get back to a previous selection.
